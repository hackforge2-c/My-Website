Frequently Asked Questions
Is HackForge free to use? ›
Yes — HackForge is completely free to access. All notes, roadmaps, and learning materials are available without payment.
Can I contribute content or suggest topics? ›
Absolutely. Send us a message using the form above with your suggestion. We review all contributions.
I found a bug. How do I report it? ›
Use the contact form above and select "Bug Report". Include the page URL, browser, and a description of the issue.
Is the content suitable for complete beginners? ›
Yes. HackForge is designed for all levels — from absolute beginners to intermediate learners. The Roadmap page shows you where to start.
How do I delete my account? ›
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account Permanently. This action cannot be undone.
